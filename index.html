Meet crodie

Drake's Cat

Dive into the captivating world of Crodie, the beloved cat of superstar Drake. Join us as we explore the extraordinary life of this elegant, green-eyed feline who has captured hearts worldwide.

Breed: Bengal
Name: Crodie

Drake has a Bengal cat named Crodie. “Crodie” or “Cro” is Toronto slang for a ride-or-die friend, which comes from a variation of “bro,” used by the Crips gang. 

When Drake first posted his cat to Instagram, he referred to her as “Cyatty,” which is a Toronto-Jamaican slang term for a “loud and obnoxious female.”
